Peer Area

Rule Area

Innovation
  • Is it an amazing new scenario ?
  • How creative is the idea ?
  • Is it a different take on an existing/similar idea ?
  • How ambitious is the hack ?
Business Value
  • Does the application create value ?
  • Does it save money or create opportunity ?
  • Does it solve a real problem ?
  • How many people does it have an impact on ?
  • Would customers use it ?
User Experience
  • Does the app have a user interface that is gorgeous, easy to use, and fast ?
  • Is it intuitive ?
Functionality
  • Does it accomplish its stated goal ?
  • Does it work? Can you demo it, not just give a PP presentation ?
  • Does the app work completely from start to finish ?
